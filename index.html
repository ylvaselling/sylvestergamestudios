Get Wrecked Insect

Get Wrecked Insect is a game developed for iOS which is played with facial gestures. The player embodies a chameleon and aims at insects with his/her mouth. By opening the mouth, the tongue of the chameleon is launced and points are earned. The aim is to survive as long as possible.

Get Wrecked Insect is developed for iOS and uses the TruDepth camera that exist in iPhone X and up. The game is developed with ARKit and Unity. Currently the game is in process to be published to App Store.
