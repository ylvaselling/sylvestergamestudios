Get Wrecked Insect

Get Wrecked Insect is a game developed for iOS which is played with facial gestures. The player embodies a chameleon and aims at insects with his/her mouth. By opening the mouth, the tongue of the chameleon is launced and points are earned. The aim is to survive as long as possible.

Get Wrecked Insect is developed for iOS and uses the TrueDepth camera that exist in iPhone X and newer models. The game is developed with ARKit and Unity. Currently the game is in process to be published to App Store.

Privacy Policy

We do not collect any data in the game. No third-party is used that collects data either. The game utilizes the TrueDepth API provided by Apple. The TrueDepth API collects information about face pose and blend shapes, which are collected in order to know the position and rotation of the face of the player, and which facial expression the player has. The facial expression and the pose of the face (position and rotation) are the variables that are used to play the game. The information is only collected and used while playing the game, since it enables playing the game. The information is not stored, nor is it shared with a third party.
